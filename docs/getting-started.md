# Getting Started

UsagePal opens a short setup window the first time you launch it.

## First Launch

The setup window shows a live miniature of the menu-bar app, walks you through two quick gestures
on a sample provider view, lets you pick exactly which pace alerts to enable, and offers to start
UsagePal when you sign in. Every step can be skipped, and everything can be changed later in
Settings.

## Providers

Sign in to Claude, Cursor, Codex, and other supported tools in their own apps. UsagePal detects local
provider data and shows the providers it can read.

To add or remove providers later, open **Settings → Plugins**. Drag providers to reorder the sidebar
and the menu-bar tray order.

## Useful Details

- Hover usage labels, warnings, and reset information to see extra detail.
- Click reset times to switch between countdown and exact time.
- Use Share to create polished images of your usage.
- Click the footer countdown to refresh manually.
